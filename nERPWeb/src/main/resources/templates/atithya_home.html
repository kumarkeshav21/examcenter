    About AtithyA

    An integrated Property Management System empowered by innovation to mange all your Hotels, Motels, Resorts, Clubs operations effortlessly. Fully integrated modules to handle Front-office, Reservation, Room Services, Maintenance, Housekeeping, Guest Engagement, Event Management, Banqueting, Laundry, Gym, Spa, Restaurants, Bar, Asset Management, Material Management, Purchase, Sales, Inventory, Account Payable/Receivable, Bank Reconciliation, HRMS, Payroll, MIS, Dashboard with in-depth analysis, Mobile check in/out and with on-line booking engine. It is empowered with Mobile App for Customers/Restaurants/Staffs to check-in/out, food/services order, Menu, Rate cards, order, kitchen notification, billing, feedback and so.
